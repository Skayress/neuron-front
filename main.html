Our technologies

Secure storage and processing of your data and documents

Decentralized AI compute

Decentralized AI compute refers to the concept of distributing artificial intelligence computing power across multiple nodes or devices. Instead of relying on a centralized system, decentralized AI compute allows for more efficient processing, increased privacy, and reduced vulnerability to single points of failure.

AI blockchain

AI blockchain refers to the combination of artificial intelligence and blockchain technology. This integration allows for the development of decentralized and secure systems that leverage AI capabilities. The use of blockchain ensures that data is stored in a tamper-proof manner, while AI enables intelligent decision-making and automation.

Decetralized secure storage

Decentralized secure storage refers to a storage system where data is distributed across multiple network nodes rather than being stored in a central location. This helps enhance security as the data is not vulnerable to a single point of failure.
